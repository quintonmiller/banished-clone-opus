Achievements

Exiled tracks achievements across all your games. Earn them once, and they stay unlocked forever. Some achievements grant per-game bonuses when earned during a session.

How Achievements Work

  • Cross-game tracking: Achievements persist across games. Your progress is saved in your browser.
  • Re-earning: Some achievements can be earned again in new games, incrementing your count.
  • Per-game bonuses: Certain achievements grant gameplay bonuses (like faster gathering or increased happiness) for the duration of the game in which they are earned.
  • Secret achievements: Some achievements are hidden — their descriptions are revealed only after unlocking them.

View your achievements from the Start Screen or Pause Menu.
